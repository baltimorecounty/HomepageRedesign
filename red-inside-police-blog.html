Thursday Morning Crash in Freeland Leaves Man Deceased
Mar 12, 2015 2:33:00 PM EDT
Posted by: John Wachter

A single vehicle crash this morning in Freeland has left one man deceased.

At 6:46 a.m. this morning, Baltimore County Police and Fire personnel responded to Middletown Road and Beckleysville Road for a report of a serious crash involving an overturned vehicle.

The initial investigation into this incident has indicated that a 1995 Dodge Neon had been traveling south-bound on Middletown Road approaching Beckleysville Road. The Neon went off the roadway, struck an embankment and several fixed objects before coming to rest on its roof.

The driver, and only occupant of the Dodge Neon, was declared deceased at the scene. He has been identified as 22-year-old David Justin Waddell of the 1000 block of Fairview Drive in Hanover, Pennsylvania.

Baltimore County Public School Teacher Charged With Sexual Abuse of a Minor
Mar 12, 2015 6:00:00 AM EDT
Posted by: John Wachter

Baltimore County Police have charged a Baltimore County Public School teacher with sexual abuse of a minor.

On December 21, 2014, Baltimore County Police learned about allegations that a teacher at General John Stricker Middle School in Dundalk had inappropriate contact with a student at the school.

The student told investigators that the contact with the teacher started in early December 2014 and involved kissing. The teacher also showed the student notes on his cell phone which stated things that included "Do you do more than a kiss?" The teacher also poked the student in areas of the student's chest and on the student's buttocks one or two times with his finger. The last time that there was contact was on December 19, 2014.

The teacher has been identified as 25-year-old Chad Aaron Hall of the Unit Block of Lincoln Woods Way 21128. He has been charged with sexual abuse of a minor, third degree sex offense, and second degree assault. He was released on $150,000 bail.

This incident remains under investigation by the Baltimore county Police Crimes Against Children Unit.

Anyone with information on Chad Aaron Hall is asked to call Baltimore County Police at 410-307-2020.

Pedestrian Injured in Catonsville Crash
Mar 12, 2015 2:00:00 AM EDT
Posted by: John Wachter

A pedestrian involved crash in Catonsville Wednesday morning has left a 15-year-old girl injured.

At 7:08 Wednesday morning, Baltimore County Police and Fire personnel responded to the Unit block of Shady Nook Avenue 21228 for a report of a crash involving a pedestrian.

The initial investigation into this incident has indicated that a 15-year-old female had been walking east-bound on a sidewalk along Blakeney Road. A 2001 Chevrolet Malibu had been traveling south-bound on Shady Nook Avenue. The pedestrian stepped off the curb, into the path of the Malibu, and was struck by it. The Malibu remained at the scene.

The pedestrian was taken to Johns Hopkins Hospital for treatment. Her current condition is unknown.

The cause of the crash has been determined to be pedestrian error.
